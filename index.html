Managua is the capital and largest city of Nicaragua, located by Lake Managua. Known for its rich history and vibrant culture, it features attractions like the National Palace of Culture and the Loma de Tiscapa viewpoint. Despite challenges like earthquakes, Managua has grown into a bustling urban center with scenic views and a taste of traditional Nicaraguan cuisine.
